What are Wizards?

Wizards are interactive workflows that guide you through complex AudioMason tasks. They can:

  • Process audiobooks with custom settings
  • Apply multiple effects in sequence
  • Configure plugin pipelines
  • Automate repetitive tasks
  • Validate and fix metadata
